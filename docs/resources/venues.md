# Conferences & Journals

This page answers a practical question: where does each kind of embodied-AI work usually surface first?

## Robotics-core venues

| Venue | Best for | Link |
|---|---|---|
| CoRL | machine learning for robotics, robot learning, VLA, manipulation, RL | https://www.corl.org/ |
| RSS | highly selective robotics systems and methods | https://roboticsconference.org/ |
| ICRA | broad robotics coverage across systems, control, manipulation, perception | https://www.ieee-ras.org/conferences-workshops/fully-sponsored/icra |
| IROS | broad robotics, systems, applications, and deployment-adjacent work | https://www.ieee-ras.org/conferences-workshops/financially-co-sponsored/iros |

## Vision-core venues

| Venue | Best for | Link |
|---|---|---|
| CVPR | perception-heavy embodied AI, manipulation perception, VLM and 3D | https://cvpr.thecvf.com/ |
| ICCV | 3D scene understanding, multimodal perception, embodied reasoning | https://iccv.thecvf.com/ |
| ECCV | 3D, video, embodied vision, affordance and grounding | https://eccv.ecva.net/ |

## ML-core venues

| Venue | Best for | Link |
|---|---|---|
| ICLR | world models, foundation models, RL, multimodal reasoning | https://iclr.cc/ |
| ICML | scalable learning systems, RL, training recipes, predictive models | https://icml.cc/ |
| NeurIPS | RL, benchmarks, large models, multimodal and predictive systems | https://neurips.cc/ |

## High-signal journals

| Journal | Best for | Link |
|---|---|---|
| IEEE RA-L | fast-turnaround robotics results and systems papers | https://www.ieee-ras.org/publications/ra-l |
| IEEE T-RO | archival robotics work with system depth | https://www.ieee-ras.org/publications/t-ro |
| IJRR | long-form robotics work and mature technical stories | https://journals.sagepub.com/home/ijr |
| Science Robotics | high-visibility robotics milestones | https://www.science.org/journal/scirobotics |

## Practical reading rule

- Watch `CoRL`, `ICLR`, `ICML`, and `NeurIPS` for field direction.
- Watch `RSS`, `ICRA`, `IROS`, and `RA-L` for grounded robotics systems.
- Watch `CVPR`, `ICCV`, and `ECCV` when the topic is perception, affordance, 3D, or multimodal grounding.
