# Affordance Paper List

Curated starting list for affordance learning and actionable parts.

| Paper | Venue / Year | Focus | Links |
|---|---|---|---|
| AffordanceNet | ICRA 2018 | 2D object-affordance detection | [Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/1709.07326) · [Code](https://github.com/nqanh/affordance-net) |
| Where2Act | 2021 | pixels-to-actions for articulated objects | [Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2101.02692) · [Code](https://github.com/daerduoCarey/where2act) |
| VAT-Mart | ICLR 2022 | trajectory-aware affordance for articulated interaction | [Project](https://hyperplane-lab.github.io/vat-mart/) |
| 3D-AffordanceNet | CVPR 2021 | canonical 3D affordance benchmark | [Project](https://andlollipopde.github.io/3D-AffordanceNet/) · [Code](https://github.com/Gorilla-Lab-SCUT/AffordanceNet) |
| GAPartNet | CVPR 2023 Highlight | actionable parts and cross-category perception/manipulation | [Project](https://pku-epic.github.io/GAPartNet/) · [Code](https://github.com/PKU-EPIC/GAPartNet) |
| PartManip | CVPR 2023 | cross-category part manipulation from point clouds | [Code](https://github.com/PKU-EPIC/PartManip) |
| AdaAfford | 2021 | few-shot adaptation of articulated-object affordance | [Project](https://hyperplane-lab.github.io/AdaAfford) · [Code](https://github.com/wangyian-me/AdaAffordCode) |
| Robo-ABC | 2024 | affordance transfer beyond categories via semantic correspondence | [Paper](https://arxiv.org/html/2401.07487v1) |
