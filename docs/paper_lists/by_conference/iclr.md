# ICLR

## Why watch ICLR

ICLR is still one of the best venues for representation learning, world models, VLA design, embodied reasoning, and robot-policy scaling ideas.

## Official entry

https://iclr.cc/

## Curation note

This page focuses on ICLR 2026 accepted papers that are directly useful for embodied AI readers, and avoids repeating the most obvious overlaps already highlighted in the roadmap pages.

## 2026 embodied AI paper picks

- [Embodied-R1: Reinforced Embodied Reasoning for General Robotic Manipulation](https://openreview.net/forum?id=i5wlozMFsQ)
  ICLR 2026 poster. Good paper if you want to track embodied reasoning as a bridge between scene understanding and action.

- [WholeBodyVLA: Towards Unified Latent VLA for Whole-body Loco-manipulation Control](https://openreview.net/forum?id=OCJmVjyzN7)
  ICLR 2026 poster. High-signal paper for readers who care about whole-body VLA rather than arm-only manipulation.

- [Interleave-VLA: Enhancing Robot Manipulation with Image-Text Interleaved Instructions](https://openreview.net/forum?id=ULTWUuGhC3)
  ICLR 2026 poster. Worth reading if you think robot instruction interfaces should move beyond plain text.

- [RoboInter: A Holistic Intermediate Representation Suite Towards Robotic Manipulation](https://openreview.net/forum?id=PGUC3mmMoi)
  ICLR 2026 poster. Useful because it adds a benchmark-and-representation layer for plan-then-execute manipulation.

- [MemoryVLA: Perceptual-Cognitive Memory in Vision-Language-Action Models for Robotic Manipulation](https://openreview.net/forum?id=54U3XHf7qq)
  ICLR 2026 poster. A good read if your interest is long-horizon manipulation and temporal memory rather than one-step control.

- [TwinVLA: Data-Efficient Bimanual Manipulation with Twin Single-Arm Vision-Language-Action Models](https://openreview.net/forum?id=jG9W6nAwVz)
  ICLR 2026 poster. Strong candidate if you want modular bimanual learning without assuming large proprietary bimanual corpora.
