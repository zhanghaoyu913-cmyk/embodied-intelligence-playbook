# ICRA

## Why watch ICRA
ICRA is the broadest mainstream robotics venue. It is where many manipulation, grasping, benchmark, and systems papers land.

## Official entry
https://www.ieee-ras.org/conferences-workshops/fully-sponsored/icra

## Suggested embodied-AI watchlist
- Open X-Embodiment (ICRA 2024 best paper ecosystem): https://robotics-transformer-x.github.io/
- ICRT (ICRA 2025): https://icrt.dev
- Track keywords: manipulation, teleoperation, grasping, world model, VLA, humanoid, sim-to-real
