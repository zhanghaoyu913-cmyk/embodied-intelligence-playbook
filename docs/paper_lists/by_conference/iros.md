# IROS

## Why watch IROS

IROS is broad, system-heavy, and often more deployment- and application-facing than the ML-first venues. It is especially useful for following whole systems, deformable manipulation, service robotics, and field-facing embodied work.

## Official entry

https://www.ieee-ras.org/conferences-workshops/financially-co-sponsored/iros

## Curation note

As of April 18, 2026, the cleanest public trail for many 2025 embodied-manipulation papers around IROS is through official project pages and official IROS workshop OpenReview entries. This page therefore uses those public entry points rather than pretending the discoverability problem does not exist.

## 2025 embodied AI paper picks

- [MetaFold: Language-Guided Multi-Category Garment Folding Framework via Trajectory Generation and Foundation Model](https://meta-fold.github.io/)
  IROS 2025 oral. Strong choice if you want a modern garment-manipulation paper with both dataset and method value.

- [DexGarmentLab: Dexterous Garment Manipulation Environment with Generalizable Policy](https://openreview.net/forum?id=7d9mumQ4ur)
  IROS 2025 workshop-sidecar. Good environment paper for readers who care about dexterous deformable-object manipulation rather than rigid-only benchmarks.

- [GarmentPile: Point-Level Visual Affordance Guided Retrieval and Adaptation for Cluttered Garments Manipulation](https://openreview.net/forum?id=7KmgcjtXfL)
  IROS 2025 workshop-sidecar. Useful because it studies cluttered garment settings where point-level affordance actually changes downstream manipulation.

- [Scene Understanding in Deformable Object Manipulation via Taxonomy-Guided Vision-Language Models](https://openreview.net/forum?id=ZENyCqjkxa)
  IROS 2025 workshop-sidecar. Worth following if you care about structured reasoning for deformable-object scenes instead of end-to-end policy-only approaches.

- [3D Shape Tracking of Deformable Linear Objects via Tactile-Visual Fusion for Collaborative Assembly](https://openreview.net/forum?id=kIvs6XENKi)
  IROS 2025 workshop-sidecar. A strong embodied-sensing paper for cable-like objects and collaborative assembly.
