# ICCV

## Why watch ICCV

ICCV is often where 3D vision, egocentric understanding, persistent memory, and fine-grained affordance reasoning connect most directly to embodied use cases.

## Official entry

https://iccv.thecvf.com/

## 2025 embodied AI paper picks

- [Learning Precise Affordances from Egocentric Videos for Robotic Manipulation](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2025/html/Li_Learning_Precise_Affordances_from_Egocentric_Videos_for_Robotic_Manipulation_ICCV_2025_paper.html)
  ICCV 2025. Strong embodied-vision paper because it goes all the way from egocentric data to real-world affordance-oriented grasping.

- [Embodied VideoAgent: Persistent Memory from Egocentric Videos and Embodied Sensors Enables Dynamic Scene Understanding](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2025/html/Fan_Embodied_VideoAgent_Persistent_Memory_from_Egocentric_Videos_and_Embodied_Sensors_ICCV_2025_paper.html)
  ICCV 2025. Worth reading if you care about memory, dynamic scenes, and embodied sensory fusion rather than only static perception.

- [EC-Flow: Enabling Versatile Robotic Manipulation from Action-Unlabeled Videos via Embodiment-Centric Flow](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2025/html/Chen_EC-Flow_Enabling_Versatile_Robotic_Manipulation_from_Action-Unlabeled_Videos_via_Embodiment-Centric_ICCV_2025_paper.html)
  ICCV 2025. Useful for readers tracking unlabeled-video-to-manipulation pipelines.

- [AffordDexGrasp: Open-set Language-guided Dexterous Grasp with Generalizable-Instructive Affordance](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2025/html/Wei_AffordDexGrasp_Open-set_Language-guided_Dexterous_Grasp_with_Generalizable-Instructive_Affordance_ICCV_2025_paper.html)
  ICCV 2025. Good paper if you care about open-set dexterous grasping with language and affordance rather than only closed-set object categories.

- [EmbodiedOcc: Embodied 3D Occupancy Prediction for Vision-based Online Scene Understanding](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2025/html/Wu_EmbodiedOcc_Embodied_3D_Occupancy_Prediction_for_Vision-based_Online_Scene_Understanding_ICCV_2025_paper.html)
  ICCV 2025. A good scene-understanding paper for readers who want online embodied mapping rather than offline multi-view reconstruction.
