# ECCV

## Why watch ECCV

ECCV is a strong venue for 3D perception, egocentric understanding, object interaction, and fine-grained scene reasoning that later feeds embodied systems.

## Official entry

https://eccv.ecva.net/

## Status note

As of April 18, 2026, the official ECCV 2026 accepted-paper list is not public yet. To avoid inventing acceptances, this page tracks a short pre-ECCV 2026 staging watchlist of recent 2026 embodied-vision preprints that are worth monitoring.

## 2026 pre-ECCV watchlist

- [Pandora: Articulated 3D Scene Graphs from Egocentric Vision](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.28732)
  March 2026 preprint. Strong fit for ECCV-style embodied perception because it turns human egocentric exploration into articulated scene graphs that help downstream mobile manipulation.

- [PRISM: A Multi-View Multi-Capability Retail Video Dataset for Embodied Vision-Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.29281)
  March 2026 preprint. Worth watching if you care about domain-specific embodied VLM evaluation in real deployment settings.

- [RAAP: Retrieval-Augmented Affordance Prediction with Cross-Image Action Alignment](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.29419)
  March 2026 preprint. A good affordance paper for readers interested in low-data transfer and retrieval-augmented grounding.

- [AffordSim: A Scalable Data Generator and Benchmark for Affordance-Aware Robotic Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.11674)
  April 2026 preprint. High-signal benchmark and data-generation paper for affordance-aware manipulation.
