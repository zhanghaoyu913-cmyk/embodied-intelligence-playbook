# ICML

## Why watch ICML

ICML is where broader learning ideas with downstream embodied implications often land first: test-time adaptation, world models, benchmarks, memory, reward learning, and cross-domain transfer.

## Official entry

https://icml.cc/

## 2025 embodied AI paper picks

- [World Model Implanting for Test-time Adaptation of Embodied Agents](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v267/yoo25a.html)
  ICML 2025. A useful read if you care about adapting embodied agents at test time without fully retraining them.

- [SAM2Act: Integrating Visual Foundation Model with A Memory Architecture for Robotic Manipulation](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v267/fang25c.html)
  ICML 2025. Strong paper for readers tracking memory-augmented manipulation policies built on visual foundation models.

- [BiAssemble: Learning Collaborative Affordance for Bimanual Geometric Assembly](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v267/shen25i.html)
  ICML 2025. Worth reading if you want a bimanual affordance paper that is grounded in assembly rather than generic affordance maps.

- [Multi-Stage Manipulation with Demonstration-Augmented Reward, Policy, and World Model Learning](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v267/escoriza25a.html)
  ICML 2025. Useful for long-horizon visual manipulation because it combines demos, reward learning, and world models instead of relying on one ingredient only.

- [EmbodiedBench: Comprehensive Benchmarking Multi-modal Large Language Models for Vision-Driven Embodied Agents](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v267/yang25f.html)
  ICML 2025. A good benchmark paper for readers trying to compare embodied-agent reasoning rather than only policy success on a single environment.
