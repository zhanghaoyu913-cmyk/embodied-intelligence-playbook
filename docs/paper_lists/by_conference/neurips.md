# NeurIPS

## Why watch NeurIPS

NeurIPS matters for embodied AI when the contribution is really about scalable learning systems, generative models, efficient inference, synthetic data, or reasoning-heavy control.

## Official entry

https://neurips.cc/

## 2025 embodied AI paper picks

- [RoboScape: Physics-informed Embodied World Model](https://openreview.net/forum?id=wbZCBBrq3W)
  NeurIPS 2025 spotlight. Strong world-model paper if you care about physically plausible video generation for manipulation.

- [ForceVLA: Enhancing VLA Models with a Force-aware MoE for Contact-rich Manipulation](https://openreview.net/forum?id=2845H8Ua5D)
  NeurIPS 2025 poster. Useful because it pushes force into the VLA stack rather than treating contact as a hidden execution detail.

- [OWMM-Agent: Open World Mobile Manipulation With Multi-modal Agentic Data Synthesis](https://openreview.net/forum?id=vSLzoUoJt6)
  NeurIPS 2025 poster. High-signal paper for readers tracking mobile manipulation plus agentic data synthesis.

- [VLA-Cache: Efficient Vision-Language-Action Manipulation via Adaptive Token Caching](https://openreview.net/forum?id=QZYZ0Xm58q)
  NeurIPS 2025 poster. Good to read if you care about real-time inference constraints rather than just raw benchmark accuracy.

- [VideoVLA: Video Generators Can Be Generalizable Robot Manipulators](https://openreview.net/forum?id=UPHlqbZFZB)
  NeurIPS 2025 poster. Worth following if you are interested in the convergence of video generation and manipulation policy learning.
